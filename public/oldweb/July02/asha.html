Asher's Page
Updated 11/07/2002

Asher is almost nine months old, and finds life very enjoyable.

Below is a summary of Asher's current thinking:

Clapping
Asher loves all music, and will clap along, especially if other people around him are clapping too.

Eating
Asher is very much in favour of eating, and will eat virtually anything.

Splashing
Asher loves having a bath and splashes as wide an area as possible.

Quiet
Asher is a quiet baby. He doesn't get very upset with anything except strangers and being left alone in a room.

Smiling
Asher does this a lot. If he's not sure of a new thing he will smile at it, and hope it will be nice to him.

Sitting Up
Asher is a clever boy at this. Rarely now does he topple over, and even then he smiles.
