    Dawkins arrived at our house at the end of October 1998. He travelled in a cardboard box, with his new friend Chico. Dawkins is seen here just after he left his hutch, and saw his new home for the first time.

    He is only about eight weeks old in this photo, so he's a little bigger now.
    [top]



    We'd already got a hutch for them to live in.
    It can be seen here on a sunny afternoon.

    We painted their names on it in case they got lost, and didn't know where they lived.

    [top]





    They also have a run, or ark, which they inhabit on occasion.
    We painted it yellow, so that they could both have a running theme in their lives, and would feel more secure (or at least, slightly important).

    It can be seen here on another sunny day.

    [top]






    Dawkins occasionally comes into the house, but he prefers to be outdoors.
