Dawkins' Page
Dawkins' pages last updated: 9/3/99
Dawkins is a rabbit that lives with us, in a hutch in the garden. He has an elevated position. He is a black Netherland Dwarf rabbit, and hops (when allowed) around our garden.

Dawkins has a happy life (we hope), and is particularly keen on not getting caught. This is usually when we're about to go out and have to put him back into the hutch. Gently he hops away from our outstretched arms. He never goes far away, but he's always just out of reach. Dawkins rather brief photo collection is now on-line.

Dawkins is named after Richard Dawkins.
