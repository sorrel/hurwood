Heidi's Flower Page
Although I do love wild flowers I don't actually do with them what I'm doing in the drawing. Remember kids, it's not big or clever to rip them out of the ground, and throw them over your head (while dancing). It doesn't help them very much. I love wild flowers because they grow everywhere (apart from concrete places), and because there's so many different kinds.
I used to find all the ones pictured here around Burgess Hill, in Sussex, when I went walking there. These were all in the fields around the shetland ponies and shetland foals that I fed apples to. These are my favourite three wild flowers at the moment - Cowslip, Harebell and Scarlet Pimpernel.
