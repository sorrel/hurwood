Indigo's World of Dogs

Page last updated on the 26th of May 2006

 

Indigo would this time like to present a few of her favourite dogs. Dogs are now her very favourite things ever: she's offically mad on dogs.

Indigo's favourite book

Puppy Love

Indigo particularly likes the book Puppy Love at the moment, as it features several puppies, and their stories.

 

Pongo

Beyond books, Indigo has gathered together a few of her favourite dogs on this page, and hopes to share a few things about them with you.

She has also written her first story, called Dogs on the Launch, which is presented further down the page.

Indigo would like to add that she likes dogs because they're pretty, and because some of them are spotty. She also particularly likes spaniels.

 

Indigo and her canine friends

Pongo

Pongo is a Dalmatian. Dalmation's are Indigo's favourite breed of dog because they're spotty.

This one has a puppy, called Lucky, and he can be seen in the picture of Indigo with all her most favourite dogs, towards the left.

Sizzle

Sizzle is Indigo's absolute favourite of all. He's soft, and he's quite friendly. He's a sausage dog.

He jumps at at you when you stroke him, even though he's only a toy.

Sizzle

Patio

He's the lying down one. He's called Patio because he likes to be patted. He's got two patches over his eyes.

 

Patio

Rascal

Indigo's Story

When asked for some more contribution towards her web-page, Indigo said she would write a story. She said it would be called Dogs on the Launch. No-one is quite sure, and the writer can't remember, quite why the word launch is in the title; but that is the title, and the word is staying there!

So, without further ado, here is Indigo's first story.

 

Dogs on the Launch

Once upon a time a dog named Caroline went to Dog School. A Dog named Hurwood lived at number 28 and went to school as well.

A cat named Charley went to school as well. They all had a big kufullfle-ruffle type of thing.

The cat was arguing so much he nearly fell over. The dog didn't want to go to school ever. So they never went to school again.

The End.

 

And that concludes the story, and also concludes Indigo's page.
