Indigo's Undersea World

Page last updated on the 4th of January 2004

Indigo has a great love of sea creatures, and this month we wondered if she wanted to share some of her thoughts about sea creatures with the world.

Indigo has a new Sea Creature curtain

New sea creature curtain

Indigo loves her new sea creature curtain, which was painted by Mummy over several nights. Indigo's previous pastoral curtain has been kindly donated to Asher.

Daddy: What do you like best about your new curtain?

Indigo: I like the jelly fish. Wiggle wiggle! But not the sting. Tell you what - I'll be the jelly fish, and I'll sting you!

Indigo runs behind Daddy and repeatedly jabs him.

Indigo: Sting! Sting! Sting!

Octopus

Indigo has a new soft-toy octopus friend, called peach, which she chose over a Jellyfish toy she also wanted, one day we were all in town together.

Daddy: Do you like Peach?

Indigo: He's alright.

Daddy: You chose him over the jellyfish, didn't you. Do you prefer Octopus to Jellyfish?

Indigo: I like them both.

Sharks

Indigo likes sharks, having a particular favourite species.

Daddy: What's your favourite type of shark?

Indigo: Hammerhead.

Daddy:And what do you like about them?

Indigo: They've got hammers on their heads. One came in the house. Look there it goes!

Indigo jumps on Daddy and won't stop laughing.

A Manta Ray, yesterday

Manta Ray

Indigo's favourite sea creature for many months were Rays, particularly Manta Rays.

Daddy: Do you remember the Rays at the London Aquarium?

Indigo: Yes.

Daddy: You got a little plastic Manta Ray in the shop there.

Indigo: I haven't.

Daddy: And why do you like Rays?

Indigo: Because they're so beautiful. I like all rays.

Sea Horse

Indigo likes the little sea-horses too.

Daddy: Tell me about sea-horses. What were the ones doing that we saw at the London Aquarium?

Indigo: Eating.

Daddy: And why do you like sea-horses so much?

Indigo: Because they're pretty. Manta Rays aren't pretty - they're bitty! Bitty bitty bitty.

The inevitable Finding Nemo connection

It has not escaped Indigo's notice that a certain film was released in Britain late last year concerning aquatic inhabitants.

Daddy: Who was your favourite character from Nemo?

Indigo: Brr brr brrrrrrr.

Daddy: Ok. Who did you like best in Nemo?

Indigo: I like Dory. He's a Regal Tan. Aaaa aaaa aaaa Nemo!

Indigo has decided that running around being silly is the better option, and so runs around the room in big circles.

Indigo: Aaaa aaaa aaaaaa, Nemo Nemo Nemo!

And the interview is concluded.
