Asher's Page

Page last updated on the 25th of August 2003

This page is Asher's space, which he can fill with whatever he chooses. As he's still not brilliant at articulating what he wants at the moment, we've interviewed him again.

Asher exits, pursued by a Dalek

Daddy: Hi Asher, how are you feeling?

Asher runs off, and returns with a book.

Asher: Book.

Daddy: That's right. How have you been today?

Asher runs off, and returns with a toy bear.

Asher: Bear.

Asher sees a picture of a horse in the room.

Asher: Horsey, horsey!

Daddy: You're right Asher. Good.

Asher rushes over to a cupboard, and tries to take a playhouse out of a drawer, but fails to get it out properly.

Asher: Daddy! I can't!

Daddy: No. Shall we leave that where it is?

Asher: Indigo. Mummy!

Daddy: What else did we see today? Planes and helicopters?

Asher gestures up into the air.

Asher: Helicopter! Helicopter! Up! A dog!

Daddy: And you liked the singing, didn't you?

Asher: Yeah. I did. Daddy!

Daddy: And the stinging nettle. It hurt your hand, didn't it?

Asher holds up his hand, and examines it.

Asher: Stinging! Stinging! Where's Mummy?

Daddy: She's downstairs with Indigo.

Asher rushes into another room, and hides behind a bed.

Daddy: What are you doing?.

Asher jumps up from behind the bed.

Asher enjoys food

Asher: Here you go!

Asher jumps up again, and then hides again.

Daddy: Are you hiding?.

Asher jumps up again.

Asher: Boo! A bed!

Asher runs back to the first room.

Daddy: What animals do you like Asher?.

Asher: Bear!

Asher points to a poster that has a bear on it, and then to further objects around the room.

Asher: Bear! Telephone! Bear! Miffy! Rabbit!

Asher picks up the telephone, and talks into the receiver.

Asher: Rabbit!

Daddy: Miffy is a rabbit. That's right.

Asher continues to talk to the telephone.

Asher: Hello Daddy! Ring! Look!

Asher holds the telephone up to the bear.

Asher: Rabbit!

And the interview is concluded.
