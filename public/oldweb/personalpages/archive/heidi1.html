Why the dog?

I choose a dog not just because I like dogs for their happy demeanour, unconditional love and wet adoring eyes - but for a certain dog; a puppy I met in Belize.

The dog

I was sitting at a beach bar when I saw in the distance a fast moving shape getting rapidly closer. We were the only people there and it was making straight for us. When it arrived we saw he was a very lively skinny puppy with a tail that propellered in circles. He jumped up on me, and gazed up at me with a look so expectant of play and love that it melted my heart.

I hope he's alive and happy, and hasn't ended up as someones dinner.

Summertime

It's summer, and the garden's going wild. This is my favourite time of year, and the children are enjoying the outdoors life. The paddling pool and sandpit have merged to form a lagoon full of prehistorical creatures and oversized crabs.

Books

As for reading books, I'm referring to my giant RHS Planet Dictionary all the time because of the wild growing garden. For fiction, I'm reading The Amber Spyglass, part three of Philip Pullman's "His Dark Materials" trilogy, which I think is alright, but I wouldn't go mad on it. I'm also dipping into a book of modern poetry called Staying Alive, which is nothing to do with the 'Bee Gees'.

Music

Indigo makes me happy by singing along to the songs we play in the car. Currently she and I both enjoying listening to The Mutton Birds and The Thrills*.

*Please note that I make, not follow, the trends.
