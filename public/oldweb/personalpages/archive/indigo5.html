Indigo's World of Princesses

Page last updated on the 16th of June 2005

Indigo would this time like to present a few of her favourite things. These now consist of Princesses and Mermaids.

"Indigo next to Snow White"

Aurora

Indigo is a particular fan of Aurora (pictured further down the page). Indigo was asked to say a few words about her:

"I like her dress, and her tiara. She's the fragile princess because her stand keeps breaking - it had to be sellotaped on."

At this point, we should point out that Indigo is talking about her cardboard cut-out version of Aurora, and not a real princess.

Indigo continues: "Don't move her too much or as she'll fall over. She needs to be leant against things."

When asked to comment further on why she liked Aurora best of all, Indigo continued:

"I really like her tiara, and her bag and dress. And she's holding a flower; a rose."

Aurora: The Fragile Princess

Snow White

Snow White is another popular princess. Indigo likes:

"Her basket of flowers, and I like her dress and her hair."

Ariel

Ariel is a particular favourite of Indigo, as she is both a princess, and a Mermaid:

"Ariel lives under the sea, and King Neptune is her father. I like her because she has loads of jewellery. Look at this one, it's got diamonds in the middle of it!"

Indigo is pointing out both some of Ariel's jewellery, and some of her own that she stores in her Ariel-themed music box (pictured below).

"This one glows under the covers - it lights up when you twist it. And it goes round my neck; it's a necklace.

Indigo demonstrates her iridescent seahorse necklace for some minutes, and Daddy makes many admiring noises about how wonderful it is.

"Do you like glow in the dark things? I do. I love this necklace."

Daddy asks if Indigo has any more sea-related information to share:

"I have a lot of mermaids and sea creatures. I don't like squid, though."

Ariel, in the guise of a music box

Indigo notices that one of her multicoloured starfishes has fallen off the wall, and proceeds to stick it back up again.

"I've got my very own blu-tack; look, it's keeping the poster up, and these little starfish with eyes. Once, when I was ill I lay in bed, and all the starfish fell off, and some of them lost their eyes. Mummy was here too, and saw it. But the blu-tack keeps them on the wall now."

Daddy asks if Indigo has any other Princess or Mermaid related information she wishes to impart:

"I like their jewellery, and dresses. I want to sleep like a princess."

At this point, the starfish that Indigo had re-stuck on the wall with blu-tack, fell off again, and Indigo descended into giggles.

The interview was therefore concluded.
