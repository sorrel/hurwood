This page is Heidi's space, which she fills with whatever she wants.

What I'm up to

Now that Indigo's started afternoon nursey I'm trying to spend more time with Asher. It's not too long before he starts going to pre-school, so I'm thinking about what to do when I have some time to myself, and maybe something that I can make to help bring in some money.

Heidi is forced to love Doctor Who

I'd like to have a go a stained glass or some sort of glass related craft, so I'm spending my time reading books about it at the moment. I wouldn't mind doing something involving gardening either; hopefully someone out there wants glass scultptured mossiacs covered with trailing plants!

Music

Not much has changed in my music tastes. It's pretty much just the Mutton Birds, and nothing else. I have been enjoying the new Belle and Sebastian CD Dear Catastrophe Waitress, and Duncan's been playing the Russian Marc Almond CD Heart on Snow while I'm in the room. Does that count?

Autumn

November really isn't my favourite time of the year, but it's nice to look forward to Christmas when you have young children around. I'm in the process of making them a (Blue Peter!) advent calendar, which can be seen in it's unfinished state on this page!

Telly and stuff

I'm pleased to finally be watching Brideshead Revisited on DVD, as I really enjoyed the book. To be honest the DVD makes the story seem that much more miserable, as Sebastian on the screen looks much worse when drunk than I imagined him. If Indigo had been a boy she might have been called Sebastian, but then there'd be too many Sebastians on this page, so maybe it's best that she wasn't!
