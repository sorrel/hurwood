Asher's Page

Page last updated on the 6th of July 2003

This page is Asher's space, which he can fill with whatever he chooses. As he's not brilliant at articulating what he wants at the moment, we've interviewed him.

Asher at dinner

Daddy: Hi Asher

Asher runs behind the curtains and hides.

Daddy: Do you want to play football?

Asher: Yeah. Alright?

Asher runs and gets a soft football.

Asher: Want football?

Asher throws the football at Daddy, who throws it back.

Asher: Catch!

Asher throws the ball at Daddy again.

Daddy: Shall we roll the ball?

Asher rolls the ball along the ground.

Daddy: How about we roll Asher?

Daddy rolls Asher along the ground.

Asher: Away!

Asher rolls on his own, over to some books, and brings one to Daddy.

Daddy: What's this Asher?

Asher in the doorway

Daddy points to a picture of a pig.

Asher: Cow.

Daddy: It's a pig, Asher. Pig.

Asher turns to a representation of a star.

Asher: Star!

Daddy: Good boy, Asher. Star.

Asher: Rolling, rolling.

Asher rolls a ball away.

Asher: Football, football. Catch!

Asher throws the ball at Daddy, who catches.

Daddy: Daddy caught the ball, Asher. That's enough football now.

Daddy throws the ball back into its box.

Asher: Gone!

Asher gets a shape sorter, and throws it down at Daddy.

Asher: Alright?

Daddy and Asher sort some shapes together, and the interview is over.
