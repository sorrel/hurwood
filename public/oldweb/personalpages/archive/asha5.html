A day out with Thomas & Friends

Asher recently discovered the joys of being a friend of Thomas.

Pictured here are some images from our recent day out to the magical island of Sodor.

Asher was asked to comment on each of the pictures, but this met with little success.

The conversation is reproduced below:

The Triumvirate: Asher, Daddy and Thomas

Daddy: Did you like meeting the Fat Controller?

Asher says nothing, and runs in circles.

Daddy: What did you think of meeting him?

Asher (while continuing to run): I don't know how to think.

Daddy (trying again): Did you like the day we went and saw all the engines?

Asher : I can't answer.

Daddy : Why's that?

Asher : Because this hurts, and this thing hurts, and this thing hurts, and this thing hurts.

Asher points to his head, his eye, his tummy and his foot.

Asher went into Thomas' Cab, and pulled his whistle

Daddy : Do they hurt because you keep running?

Asher : No.

Asher continues running.

Daddy : Anything you'd like to add about Thomas and friends, at all?

Asher shrugs his shoulders, and walks out of the room.

Asher returns, holding some Thomas the Tank Engine books.

Asher : Daddy you were going to read me "Duck's in charge". You haven't done that one for ages. And this book. You've not done all of this one either!

Toby was also there; He is also loved

Daddy : Alright. Which one shall I read?

Asher : I want you to really read "Safe and What".

Daddy : What's "Safe and What"?

Asher (louder): "SAFE AND WHAT!".

Daddy opens a Thomas book, and attempts to elucidate the meaning of the conversation.

Asher : Safe is in this one. With "Promise". "Safe and Promise" - they go together. Look! A claw! It's a bit like my crane with my yellow claw that I've got.

Asher then lists all the Thomas trains he owns, and then all the trains that he wishes to own.

And the conversation is concluded.
