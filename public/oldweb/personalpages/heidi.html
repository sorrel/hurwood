The Garden

It's the lovely time of year when the temperature goes up and all the plants in the garden go growing crazy. My favourite plant at this time of year has got to be Cow Parsley frothing along the borders of roads.

At this time of year there are a lot of plants already in flower, but around the corner is the promise of Summer, and a garden full of life and buzzing with insects.

Veg Patch

I am hoping that my veggies will succeed this year; it's my second year of growing and I know what I'm doing a bit more - though slugs and torrential rain have put pay to any chance of lettuces.

Last year was tomato madnes, so I'm pricking out this year and keeping only the good 'uns. In the garden, I was surprised to see many young tomato and cucumber plants sprouting up from last years' fallen fruit. I'm growing pretty much the same as last year, but with the addition of parsnips!

Fingers crossed that come August we'll be enjoying the bounty of the garden - bugs 'n' all!
