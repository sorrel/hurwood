Indigo's Page
Updated 20/07/2001

Indigo is one and a quarter, and therefore needs a little help in setting up her web page.

She has just learned to walk, and is enjoying the new freedom it gives her.

She is also learning to talk. Her first word was star.

Now she knows many more words, like chocolate and chips which we tried to stop her learning, without success.

Below is a summary of Indigo's current thinking:

Music
Indigo's favourite song is Twinkle, Twinkle little Star.

Thoughts
Indigo's current favourite pastime is going up and down stairs. She also thinks about chocolate a lot.

Books
Indigo's favourite book is currently A Day at the Farm.

Television
Indigo's has made no judgement on television programmes, but laughs when the daleks talk.

Places
Indigo likes the splash pool at the swimming pool, and being in wide open fields where she can run around.

Things
Indigo likes cats and will relentlessly try to grab them. She also likes flowers.

Eating
Indigo is not keen on this activity, unless some of her favourite words are involved.
