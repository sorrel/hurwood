Asher's Page
Updated 02/10/2002

Asher is almost a year old now, and finds life enjoyable, but frustrating.

Below is an interview conducted with Asher by Daddy, on the 2nd of October:

You've got a cold Asher, are you ok?
Ga Ga.

     (Asher sits on the bed looking worried, then happy.)

Are you feeling better after dinner?
Da Da. Da Da. Da Dee.

     (Asher crawls to the edge of the bed, and is stopped by Daddy.)

Have you had a happy day?
Aaaaaa. Aaaaa.

     (Asher grabs Daddy's foot.)

Can you do clapping?
Aaaaa. Aaaaa.

     (Asher crawls to Daddy, while Daddy claps.)

Clapping. Like this.

     (Asher crawls to Daddy, and dribbles a bit on the notebook. Asher tries to grab the pen.)

That's Daddy's pen. NO. Say "Daddy".
Da Der. Da Der.

     (Asher reaches for the pen again.)

Aaaas saaa.

Clever boy. Asher. Asher.

     (Asher tries to grab the pen again.)

Shall we do clapping?

     (Asher starts crying because he can't get the pen. Daddy stands Asher up, and he changes to instant happiness, and the interview is terminated.)
