Indigo's Page
Updated 02/10/2002

Indigo is two and a half years old now.

She enjoys playing with her toy animals, being read to, and wants to do everything, right now. Or she'll scream.

Below is an interview with Indigo, conducted by Daddy on the 2nd of October 2002:

Hi Indigo. How are you feeling today?
I'm feeling much better.

What have you done today?
I've rided on the cars at the playgroup. Can I go on the computer now?

In a minute. Where do you live?
In Mummy's house. I live at Grandmas.

Who's your best friend?
Myrtle.

    (Myrtle is Indigo's toy rabbit.)

Myrtle's on the computer. He wants to sit with me.

    (Indigo and Myrtle start typing on the keyboard.)

Myrtle types "D".

Where do you want to go on holiday?
I want to go to the seaside. With Myrtle.

     (Indigo types with vigour.)

What do you want to be when you grow up?
I want to be Asher.

What's your favourite animal?
Elephant.

Why do you like Elephants?
I do. El - Ee - PHANT!

Do you like having dinner?
I like egg-i-weggs.

Do you like playing outdoors?
I want to type with Myrtle.

What do you like playing with outside?
I like playing with my pushchair. I'm typing. I type "D". I'm on the internet. Myrtle's watching me type. He's very good at typing.

Are you a good girl?
Yeah.

Could you do anything to be a better girl?
Yeah.

What's that then?
I could dance.

     (Indigo starts bashing the mouse on the keyboard.)

Please don't bash Daddy's things.
I'm just going in the bathroom.

Bye Bye
Bye.

     (Indigo goes, then comes back holding two bottles full of coloured liquid.)

I've got these.

     (Indigo is holding food colouring. It is all over her hands.)

The interview is ended. Thank you for your time Indigo.
